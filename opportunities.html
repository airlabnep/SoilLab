Join Our Team

We are looking for passionate researchers, students, and professionals to join our lab. Whether you're interested in graduate research mentorship, undergraduate research, field work, or collaboration, we have opportunities for you.

Deep Learning Researcher

Join us for Computer Vision based Project on Sunflower. We are building HelioNet to detect the diameter and seeds of the sunflower heads. Work on cutting-edge technology for precision agriculture project with dedicated mentorship from our faculty.

Undergraduate Research

Get hands-on research experience as an undergraduate. Contribute to ongoing projects on 'UAV based crop phenotyping and assessment of enriched biochar on Maize' while developing your skills in soil science, drones, and research methods.

Collaboration Opportunities

We welcome collaborative research with other institutions, organizations, and research groups. We are focused on precision agriculture, soil health, and Deep Learning techniques in agriculture Contact us to discuss potential partnerships.

Field Research Assistants

Help us conduct field experiments and data collection. Seasonal and full-time positions available for those interested in hands-on agricultural research.

Lab Assistant

For people interested in Soil sample analysis and lab work are welcome to join our lab. We offers 3 months of internship where you will learn soil sampling techniques and use lab equipment.

Short-term Internships

Participate in our summer or semester-long internship programs. Gain valuable experience in soil science, lab work, and field research.

Application Process

To apply for any of these positions, please send your:

  • CV/Resume
  • Cover letter
  • References (2-3 contacts)
